About Me

Hi, I am Dakota Lewis, a dual-degree junior at the University of Michigan, studying information, art & design. Specializing in human-computer interaction, UX /UI Design, and data visualization

My passion for design is rooted in my desire to live in a comfortable world and derived from the perspective that good design creates products that enhance the way we live and improve the world we live in. This requires an understanding of the logos, ethos, and pathos of the worlds consumers and using aspects of design and information to assimilate the best possible outcome. The bridge between design and information is centered around user experience. In order to maximize the level of satisfaction in user experience, I believe one must center the structure and function of a product around ethics, emotion, and logic of the product's target market. Furthermore, you can only understand your consumer through the research, data, and analysis of information.
